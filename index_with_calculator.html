Permit requirements depend on the scope of work:

  • • Small amounts (under 10m²): No permit required, but must use licensed removalist
  • • Large amounts (over 10m²): Removal permit required from local council
  • • Friable asbestos: Always requires permit and Class A licensed removalist
  • • Commercial properties: Additional workplace safety notifications required

We handle all permit applications and regulatory compliance as part of our service. Our team ensures full compliance with Queensland regulations.

Properties built before 1990 are likely to contain asbestos. Common locations include:

  • • Roofing: Corrugated cement sheets, gutters, downpipes
  • • Walls: Fibro sheeting, internal linings
  • • Floors: Vinyl tiles, sheet flooring, adhesives
  • • Insulation: Pipe lagging, ceiling insulation
  • • Fencing: Cement sheet fencing

Never attempt to identify asbestos yourself. Professional testing is required for accurate identification.

We provide comprehensive asbestos inspections and testing services. Our certified inspectors will identify all asbestos-containing materials and provide detailed reports.
