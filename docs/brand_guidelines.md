## Logo Usage Guidelines

### DO:
✅ Maintain minimum clear space around logo
✅ Use on appropriate background colors
✅ Scale proportionally
✅ Use high-resolution files for print
✅ Use monochrome version for single-color applications

### DON'T:
❌ Stretch or distort the logo
❌ Change colors arbitrarily
❌ Add effects or shadows
❌ Use on busy backgrounds without proper contrast
❌ Separate text from symbol in primary logo
